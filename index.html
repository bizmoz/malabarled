Incorporated in the year 2004 at Kozhikode, Kerala, we “Malabar LED House” are a Sole Proprietorship (Individual) based firm, engaged as the Manufacturer and Supplier of LED Display, Indoor & Outdoor LED Video Wall, LED Sign, Acrylic LED Letters, Steel & Brass LED Letters, Pixel LED Letters, LED Back Light Board Token Display, P10 Modules, Control Cards, Sign LED’s & LED Power Supply . The products we manufacture are hugely acclaimed for their utmost quality. Under the skilled and specialized guidance of “Shameer C (Proprietor)” we have gained name and fame in the market.

OUR TEAM

Our company has a skilled team of experienced professionals who are highly motivated about achieving goals of the organization. We conduct various training workshops on regular basis to enhance the skills of our work force and help them to achieve a better learning experience. With our team effort we have gained amendable success in the industry. With the help of our team we are able to get more clients throughout the world.

QUALITY ASSURANCE

Our reckoned company believes in developing and supplying high quality products with quality centric approach. Our products are quality checked by our quality experts who take care of the quality of the products and regularly checked these products on the basis of various parameters set by the industry. From the very first stage of production we check the quality of products and then deliver these to our valuable clients. These products are closely inspected by the quality controller working for us as per international standards. Moreover, we always quality check of the products so that no defect occurs at the clients’ end.
